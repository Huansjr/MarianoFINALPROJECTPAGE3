Alec Benjamin is an American singer-songwriter from Phoenix, Arizona. He is known for his narrative-driven indie-pop sound and heartfelt lyrics.

Benjamin gained popularity through his songs on platforms like YouTube and SoundCloud. His breakout single, "Let Me Down Slowly", gained widespread attention and chart success.

He has released several EPs and albums, including "Narrated for You and "These Two Windows." Alec Benjamin's music often explores themes of love, relationships, and personal struggles.

Aside from his solo work, Benjamin has collaborated with artists like Alessia Cara and has toured internationally, gaining a dedicated fanbase around the world.

His distinctive voice, storytelling style, and emotional depth continue to captivate audiences, making him a rising star in the music industry.
